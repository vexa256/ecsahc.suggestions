Suggestions Collection Process

ECSA-HC has in place processes and structures for receiving suggestions and complaints

General Public

Our structure accommodates feedback and suggestions from the general public

Vendors

Our vendors are also catered for in our suggestions and feedback process

Partners

Cooperating-partners are also catered for in the ECSA-HC suggestions process

The ECSA-HC Suggestions mechanism

Our mechanism entails receiving and processing suggestions and complaints from individuals or groups positively/negatively affected by activities of a particular project, program or activity.


We have instruments, policies and structures in place which ensure that suggestions/complaints are addressed efficiently and appropriately.


We welcome suggestions/complaints that would, amongst other things, prevent the occurrence of fraud and corruption , mitigate socio-economic and environmental risks hence providing the Secretariat with practical suggestions and feedback during implementation of its region-wide programmes.

Contact

We encourage submission of suggestions or complaints through the post, verbally, over the phone (calling-in, voice-notes and texts) and emails as well as through a suggestion box at ECSA-HC headquarters. Electronic forms for submission of suggestions/complaints can be downloaded from our website, www.ecsahc.org
